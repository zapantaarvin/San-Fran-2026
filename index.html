My call

What I’d tell the family

  • Book DCA nonstop if you want the easiest airport and least stressful day-of-travel plan.
  • Book IAD nonstop if the price difference stays meaningful, especially if it saves roughly $200 to $250 total.
  • Only chase Frontier if everyone is traveling with a personal item only. Once a carry-on is added, the “cheap” fare can lose.
  • If fares spike, check SJC as a backup airport and compare against SFO before booking.
