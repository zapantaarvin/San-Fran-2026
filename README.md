# San Francisco 2026 Trip

A booking-style family trip planner for Washington, DC to San Francisco in August 2026.

## Live site

Once GitHub Pages finishes deploying, the site should be available at:

`https://zapantaarvin.github.io/San-Fran-2026/`

## What the site shows

- a carousel with `Flights`, `Stay Near SFO`, and `Itinerary` tabs
- flight duration, price guidance, and booking links
- a recommended hotel for 4 people within roughly 30 minutes of SFO
- a realistic Aug 6 to Aug 10 plan that tries to preserve family time after 4 PM on most days

## Project files

- `index.html` contains the carousel-based trip planner
- `styles.css` contains the visual design and carousel styling
- `script.js` powers the tab carousel interaction
- `.github/workflows/pages.yml` deploys the site to GitHub Pages

## Notes

The hotel recommendation and itinerary are built from current web sources and planning assumptions. Final hotel pricing, flight pricing, and travel times should always be confirmed from the booking links on the page before purchase.
